    About Us

    Nepalese Association of Central Virginia (NACV) is a non-profit organization with tax exempt status under Section 501 (c) (3) of Internal Revenue Service (IRS). It is based in Harrisonburg and surrounding area of Virginia (USA) and primarily serves the Nepali-American community in this area.
