    Message from the president

    Dear Community Members,

    Reflecting on our journey, from a small group to a growing community, it's heartening to witness our progress. With each passing day, our bond strengthens, and our numbers grow. It's time we pass on the torch of our culture to the next generation, enlightening them about the beauty and impact of our mixed cultures. Let's unite and extend our hands in unity. Together, we can nurture respect, love, and support among one another. By doing so, we'll not only strengthen our roots but also showcase to the world that we are one big family - the Nepali-American community Harrisonburg Virginia. Join us in this beautiful journey of growth and cultural preservation.

    With warm regards,
    NACV

    About Us

    Nepalese Association of Central Virginia (NACV) is a non-profit organization with tax exempt status under Section 501 (c) (3) of Internal Revenue Service (IRS). It is based in Harrisonburg and surrounding area of Virginia (USA) and primarily serves the Nepali-American community in this area.
